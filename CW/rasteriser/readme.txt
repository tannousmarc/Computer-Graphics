Some small details regarding the rasteriser:

- the initial rendered scene is the "extension" one, with the temple and dog, you can switch between this scene and the standard one with the keys T and Y
- you can move around with WASD and the mouse
- the light can be moved around with the arrow keys on the XZ axis and with ] and \ keys on the Y axis
- shadows can be activated with the key B and deactivated with the key N
- FXAA is activated with H and deactivated with J
- clipping gets activated with U and deactivated with I
- standard resolutions are 640x640 and 1000x1000
- OpenMP is deactivated by default
